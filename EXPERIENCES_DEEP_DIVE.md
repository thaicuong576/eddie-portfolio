## 10. rd-proximity - R&D Proximity - Innovation Analysis

### Problem
In emerging economies like Vietnam, do firms actually benefit from being physically close to High Technology Centers (HTCs)? While R&D is touted as a growth engine, there was a lack of empirical evidence on how geographical distance and national policies (like the 2017 Technology Transfer Law) impact the actual profitability and investment choices of listed firms.

### Solution
Conducted a high-rigor econometric study titled **"R&D funding, Firms’ Investment Decisions and Profitability: When Proximity and Law matter."** I built a longitudinal dataset spanning 14 years to analyze the knowledge spillover effects of HTCs on firm-level innovation performance.

### How it works (Flow)
- **Dataset Construction**: Aggregated and cleaned panel data from **Vietnamese listed firms (HOSE, HNX, UPCOM) from 2009–2022**.
- **Spatial Analysis**: Calculated the geographical coordinates and distance of each firm relative to major High-Tech Centers (Hanoi, HCM, Da Nang).
- **Econometric Modeling**: Applied **Panel Data Regression** models to test the interaction between R&D expenditure, geographical proximity, and the impact of the 2017 Technology Transfer Law.
- **Heterogeneity Testing**: Performed sub-group analysis across Medical, Public Utilities, Consumer Services, and FMCG industries to identify industry-specific innovation drivers.

### Results & Impact
- **Negative Distance Correlation**: Proved that geographical distance to HTCs negatively impacts R&D effectiveness; the further a firm is from a hub, the less ROI they see from their R&D spend.
- **Policy Insight**: Demonstrated that the 2017 Law significantly shifted firm investment choices toward intangible assets (patents, IP).

## 12. justfactor - JustFactor - Fintech Credit MVP

### Problem
In Vietnam, SMEs face a staggering **$24 billion funding shortfall**. Traditional banks are often unwilling to lend because the manual underwriting cost (OPEX) is disproportionately high—costing roughly $200 in administrative labor just to process a $10,000 loan. This "origination paradox" leaves 60% of SMEs without access to formal credit due to a lack of real estate collateral.

### Solution
Developed **JustFactor**, an **Intelligent Origination Platform (B2B2B)** that engineers trust between SMEs and Financial Institutions. Instead of lending directly, JustFactor acts as a data-automation layer that transforms fragmented SME invoices and tax records into standardized, bank-ready credit assets.

### How it works (Flow)
- **Data Ingestion & Verification**: SMEs upload digital invoices and connect bank accounts. The system uses **Tax Authority APIs** and **Casso (Banking APIs)** to verify transaction legitimacy in real-time.
- **The J-Score Engine**: Built a custom scoring logic in **FastAPI** that analyzes cash flow patterns and "anchor buyer" reliability to generate a standardized audit report.
- **Marketplace Matching**: Verified receivables are listed on a private dashboard where Financial Institutions can execute "True Sale" factoring transactions with 90% less manual friction.
- **Compliance Architecture**: Engineered the platform to be "Pure Platform" compliant (Decree 52/2013 & Law on Credit Institutions 2024), ensuring fund flows move directly via escrow without balance-sheet risk.

### Results & Impact
- **90% Friction Reduction**: Automated the first 80% of the credit due diligence process, significantly lowering the bank's OPEX per application.
- **Fintech Innovation**: Successfully developed the **Functional MVP** using a modern stack: **FastAPI (Python 3.11), Supabase (PostgreSQL), and React/Tailwind**.
- **Strategic Roadmap**: Designed a path for invoice fractionalization and **DeFi expansion**, allowing retail investors to eventually participate in SME credit.
